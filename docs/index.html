Why claude-pipe

Built for how you actually work

A lightweight bridge between your chat apps and a full agent runtime.

🏠

Runs on your machine

No cloud relay. Your data stays local. The agent runs where your code and credentials live.

⚡

Streaming responses

See Claude thinking in real-time. Draft messages update live as the agent works through your request.

🗂️

Workspace aware

Point it at any directory. The agent understands your project structure, reads your CLAUDE.md, and follows your conventions.

🚀

2-minute setup

Interactive wizard configures everything. Pick your channel, paste your bot token, choose a model. Done.
